Three Phase Power Measurement Using Two Wattmeter Method Under UnBalanced Loads Condition

STEP-I Make the connections as per the given instructions.

1.    A1 of Autotransformer - M1 of Wattmeter && M1 of Wattmeter - C1 of Wattmeter.

2.    L1 of Wattmeter - X1 of Ammeter && X2 of Ammeter - Y1 of load.

3.    A3 of Autotransfrormer - M2 of Wattmeter && M2 of Wattmeter - C2 of Wattmeter.

4.    L2 of Wattmeter - X5 of Ammeter && X6 of Ammeter - Y3 of Load.

5.    A2 of Autotransformer - X3 of Ammeter && X4 of Ammeter - Y2 of Load.

6.    V1 of Wattmeter - X3 of Ammeter && V2 of Wattmeter - X3 of Ammeter.

7.    Z1 of Voltmeter - A1 of Autotransformer && Z2 of Voltmeter - A2 of Autotransformer.

8.    R,B,Yand N of Mcb - R,B,Y and N of Autotransformer.

STEP-II Click on "Check" Button.

STEP-III Click on the MCB to Turn ON the circuit and Select the number of Bulbs from the Lamp Load.

STEP-IV For unbalanced load condition measured the values of wattmeters,ammeter and Voltmeter.

STEP-V Click "ADD" button to add the values to the Observation Table.

STEP VI : Add different values to the Table by selecting different number of bulbs.

STEP VII : Click on "PRINT" button to print the page.

P3Φ=3VphIphCosΦ.

Vph=VL/√3.

Where,  P3Φ=Three phase power,Vph=Phase Voltage,

Iph=Phase current, VL=Line voltage,Φ=Phase angle.
